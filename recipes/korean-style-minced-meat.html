Korean Style Minced Meat

Spicy and sweet, easy and fast to make!

Ingredients

  • 400g Minced meat
  • 150g Rice
  • 2-3 Spring Onions
  • 60ml Soy Sauce
  • 50g Dark Soft Brown Sugar
  • 1 Tbsp. Sesame Oil
  • 1 Tbsp. Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 Tsp. Ginger Powder
  • 1 Tsp. Chili Powder
  • Black pepper
  • 2 Tbsp. Black Sesame Seeds
  • Sriracha Sauce

Steps

  1. Wash and cook rice.
  2. In a bowl mix together, soy sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, garlic, ginger, chili powder and black pepper.
  3. In a pan, heat up olive or any other oil. Add white part of spring onions,fry until glazed, then add minced meat.
  4. When minced meat is good, lower heat, add soy sauce mixture and stir for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Serve meat and rice in a bowl, garnish with sriracha sauce, sesame seeds and green parts of sping onions
  6. Bonne appetit!
